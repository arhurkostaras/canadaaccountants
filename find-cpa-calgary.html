Finding the Right CPA in Calgary

Calgary's CPA market is uniquely shaped by the energy industry. The city is home to a large concentration of CPAs with expertise in oil and gas accounting, royalty calculations, energy sector tax planning, and natural resource allowances. But Calgary also has strong depth in general accounting, real estate, construction, and emerging technology sectors.

All CPAs in Alberta are regulated by CPA Alberta at cpaalberta.ca. CanadaAccountants profiles in Alberta are sourced from CPA Alberta registry data. Whether you need a generalist CPA for your small business or a specialist for your oil and gas operations, Calgary has the expertise you need.

Calgary CPA fees are competitive with other major cities. Personal returns start around $150–$450, corporate filings $1,500–$4,000 for straightforward corporations. Energy sector clients often require specialized work that commands premium rates. For ongoing accounting relationships, ask about monthly advisory packages which typically offer better value than ad hoc hourly billing.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a CPA cost in Calgary?

Calgary CPA fees are generally competitive with other Canadian cities. Personal tax returns start around $150–$450. Corporate returns for small businesses typically cost $1,500–$4,000. Monthly bookkeeping and advisory packages run $500–$2,000/month. Energy sector clients often pay premium rates for specialized oil and gas accounting.

What industries do Calgary CPAs specialize in?

Calgary CPAs often specialize in oil and gas accounting, energy sector tax, agriculture, real estate, and construction. The city's energy economy creates strong demand for CPAs with royalty calculations, resource allowances, and flow-through share expertise. Many Calgary CPAs also serve the growing tech and startup community.

Who regulates CPAs in Alberta?

CPA Alberta is the regulatory body for Chartered Professional Accountants in Alberta. You can verify any CPA's standing at cpaalberta.ca. All CanadaAccountants profiles in Alberta are sourced from CPA Alberta registry data.

Can a Calgary CPA help with oil and gas royalties?

Yes. Calgary has a large pool of CPAs with oil and gas expertise, including royalty calculations, resource allowances, Capital Cost Allowance for resource properties, and flow-through shares. When searching for a Calgary CPA, filter for those who list energy, oil and gas, or natural resources as a specialization.
