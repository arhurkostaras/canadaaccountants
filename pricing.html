Frequently Asked Questions

Why does higher pricing = higher ranking?

Because CPAs who invest more demonstrate commitment to growth and quality. It's performance-based: you pay more to be seen more. Just like Google Ads, Facebook Ads, or any marketplace with sponsored listings. The difference? We're 100% transparent about it.

Can I switch tiers later?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

What if I need more than 15 matches?

Contact us for a custom Enterprise+ plan. We'll build a custom solution for high-volume practices or firms looking to scale rapidly.

Do businesses pay anything?

No. Businesses search and connect for free. CPAs pay the subscription fee because they're building their practice.

What counts as an "active client match"?

Any business you've been matched with and are actively working with. Once a client relationship ends, that slot frees up for a new match.

Is this compliant with CPA codes?

Yes. We use a fixed-fee subscription model (not commission-based). No guarantees or promises. Full transparency. CPAs maintain professional independence. Read more in our Trust Center.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts. Cancel anytime. If you prepaid annually, you'll receive a prorated refund for unused months.

How do you verify CPA credentials?

We cross-reference with provincial CPA institutes (CPA Ontario, CPA BC, etc.) to verify active licenses before approving profiles.
