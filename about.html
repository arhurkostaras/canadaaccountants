About CanadaAccountants

Connecting Canadian businesses with qualified CPAs through AI-powered matching and verified professional data.

Our Founder

The Person Behind the Platform

AK

Arthur Kostaras

Founder & CEO

Arthur Kostaras is the founder of CanadaAccountants. With a background in financial services and technology, Arthur built CanadaAccountants to solve a simple problem: finding the right CPA shouldn't take weeks of research and networking. The platform uses AI matching and verified professional data to connect businesses with qualified CPAs across Canada.

Why We Exist

The Problem We Solve

Finding qualified accounting help in Canada is harder than it should be. We built CanadaAccountants to fix that.

Discovery Is Broken

Most businesses find a CPA through referrals or directory listings that tell you nothing about fit. Neither approach surfaces who is actually qualified for your specific situation.

AI Does the Matching

Our algorithm analyses 6 key factors — industry specialization, province, firm size, service type, designation, and availability — to surface CPAs who are genuinely qualified for your needs.

Verified Data

Every CPA in our directory is cross-referenced against provincial CPA body records. No scraped profiles, no stale data — real, active, licensed accounting professionals only.

Professional Independence

CanadaAccountants is a technology platform, not a referral agency. Every CPA maintains full professional independence and all fee arrangements remain between the CPA and the client.
